Character names in Perenthia should reflect the pseudo-medieval 
fantasy nature of the game. Please choose a character name that
reflects the persona you wish to portray.

Character names must be unique so if the one you want is already 
taken you can vary the spelling or compound another word to make
your own version of the name.

Character names must be at least three (3) letters in length and 
may not contain numbers, spaces or other special characters, with 
the exception of the apostrophe symbol (') and the dash symbol (-).
